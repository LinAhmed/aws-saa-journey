
# <img src="image-1.png" alt="EC2 Logo" width="30" height="30"> Amazon EC2

> 💡 Think of it as renting a computer in Amazon's data center that runs 24/7.

- **One of the most popular of AWS' offering** 
- **EC2 = Elastic Compute Cloud = Infrastructure as a Service**

- **It mainly consists in the capability of:**
  - **Renting virtual machines (EC2)**
    > Like leasing a laptop online, but you can choose how powerful it is (CPU, RAM, etc.).
  - **Storing data on virtual drives (EBS)**
    > Similar to an external hard drive you can attach and detach from your computer.
  - **Distributing load across machines (ELB)**
    > Like a smart receptionist directing customers to the shortest queue.
  - **Scaling the services using an auto-scaling group (ASG)**
    > automatically adding more during rush and removes them when it's quiet.
